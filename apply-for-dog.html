Apply for a Dog

Please Read Before Applying

An average NEADS dog costs over $42,000. We ask our clients to fundraise a minimum of $8,000, but please don't let this deter you from applying! NEADS will assist you by providing fundraising information and guidance.

NOTE: NEADS does not train balance dogs, seizure alert dogs, guide dogs for the blind, diabetes alert dogs, emotional support, or psychiatric dogs. NEADS does not train dogs that are already owned by individuals.
